Sensor Information at your hands!

Sensorial is an easy and simple app that allow you to see sensor information!

Sensor information can came from any part of our today lives, but one thing is for sure, we can't access that information in a very simple way and decide which data is usefull or not. The focus of Sensorial is bring this to your hands!

We use internet services to load sensor data based on your location and time, bringing in this way the most recent and update information. Also, we get information about the sensors that exists in our smartphone/computer! Few people know, but our smartphones can come with built in sensor modules beyond GPS, some phones bring pressure modules and so on.

If you enjoy our website, there's also a little app on Google Play Store, download it, and make a review and share that to your frinds!
